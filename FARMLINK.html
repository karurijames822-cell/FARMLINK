Fresh farm products at your doorstep

Farmlink is a digital platform that allows farmers to sell their agricultural products directly to the consumers while at the same time allowing consumers to access fresh farm products through internet.The journey started with a simple goal:to make high quality,local food accessible to everyone. we noticed that truly fresh produce was hard to find in stores so we decided to bridge the gap. every item inour shop is grown with care using organic fertilizers and crop rotation. we believe that healthy soil makes for healthier communities.

why choose us

our platform improves market access for both farmers and consumers,ensuring fair prices for farmers and provides customers with quality products. By shopping with us,you are supporting over 340 local growers and helping reduce carbon footprint of your groceries.

  • customers browse and place orders online
